# Girls Yellow Tennis

Your daughter trains in a supportive, girls-only environment. This advanced group uses standard balls on full courts, developing confident players ready for competitive tennis.

## What Makes Girls Yellow Special

Some girls thrive in an all-female training environment. They push each other, support each other, and grow together. The energy is focused and encouraging.

Same quality coaching as our mixed groups, with an atmosphere where girls can be themselves on court.

## What Your Daughter Will Learn

- Advanced groundstroke techniques
- Powerful and consistent serving
- Complete net play skills
- Strategic pattern play
- Mental toughness and confidence
- Competition preparation

## Session Details

- **Age:** 10+ years (skill dependent)
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Schedule:** Wednesday 5:45-6:45pm
- **Ball:** Yellow ball (standard)
- **Court:** Full court
- **Price:** $200 per 8-week term

## What to Bring

- Tennis racket (full size appropriate for your daughter)
- Water bottle
- Sunscreen and hat in summer
- Sports clothing and proper tennis shoes
