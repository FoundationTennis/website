# Yellow Group Tennis

Your child plays tennis the way it is meant to be played. Yellow Group uses standard balls on full courts, developing players who are ready for competitive tennis.

## What Makes Yellow Group Special

This is real tennis. Standard balls, full courts, proper scoring. Your child trains with players of similar ability, pushing each other to improve.

Sessions combine technical development, tactical work, and competitive match play. Players leave ready for tournaments, club competitions, and a lifetime of tennis.

## What Your Child Will Learn

- Advanced groundstroke techniques
- Powerful and consistent serving
- Complete net play arsenal
- Strategic pattern play
- Mental toughness and focus
- Competition preparation

## Session Details

- **Age:** 10+ years (skill dependent)
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Schedule:** Monday 6:30-7:30pm or Wednesday 6:45-7:45pm
- **Ball:** Yellow ball (standard)
- **Court:** Full court

### Multi-Group Option

Attend both sessions for $300/term (save $100). Serious players need serious court time.

## What to Bring

- Tennis racket (full size appropriate for your child)
- Water bottle
- Sunscreen and hat in summer
- Sports clothing and proper tennis shoes
