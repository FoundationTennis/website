# Girls Orange/Green Tennis

Your daughter develops her tennis in a supportive, girls-only environment. This group works with orange and green balls, building technique and confidence together.

## What Makes This Group Special

Learning tennis is easier when you feel comfortable. This group gives developing girls a space where they can focus on improvement without pressure.

The players encourage each other, celebrate successes together, and build friendships that extend beyond tennis.

## What Your Daughter Will Learn

- Refined groundstroke technique
- Serve development
- Net play basics
- Court movement and positioning
- Basic tactical awareness
- Match play experience

## Session Details

- **Age:** Generally 8-12 years (skill dependent)
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Schedule:** Wednesday 5-5:45pm
- **Ball:** Orange and green balls (adjusted to skill level)
- **Court:** Three-quarter to full court
- **Price:** $160 per 8-week term

## What to Bring

- Tennis racket (appropriate size for your daughter)
- Water bottle
- Sunscreen and hat in summer
- Comfortable sports clothing and shoes

## Next Steps

When your daughter is ready for standard yellow balls and wants to continue in a girls-focused group, she can progress to Girls Yellow on Wednesdays 5:45-6:45pm.
