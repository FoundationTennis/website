# After School Tennis Programs

Your child can learn tennis right at their school. We run after-school programs at two local primary schools, making it easy for young players to start their tennis journey.

## Why School Programs Work

No extra travel. No schedule juggling. Your child goes from classroom to tennis court in minutes. They play with classmates they already know, making the experience comfortable from day one.

## Paringa Park Primary School

### Program Details

- **Day:** Monday
- **Time:** 3:15-4pm
- **Location:** Paringa Park Primary School gym
- **Duration:** 8-week terms (starts week 2 of school term)
- **Ages:** 5-8+
- **Ball:** Red ball

### What Your Child Will Learn

Using soft red balls and age-appropriate equipment, your child develops:

- Basic racket handling and ball control
- Hand-eye coordination
- Simple forehand and backhand movements
- Love for tennis through fun games

## McAuley Community School

### Program Details

- **Day:** Wednesday
- **Time:** 3:15-4pm
- **Location:** McAuley Community School tennis courts
- **Duration:** 8-week terms (starts week 2 of school term)
- **Ages:** 5-8+
- **Ball:** Red ball

### What Your Child Will Learn

Same quality Foundation Tennis coaching in a familiar school environment:

- Basic tennis skills and technique
- Movement and coordination
- Game-based learning
- Fun with classmates

## What to Bring

- Tennis racket (we can provide one if needed)
- Water bottle
- Hat and sunscreen for outdoor sessions
- Comfortable shoes

## After the School Program

If your child loves tennis and wants more, they can join our regular group programs at Somerton Park Tennis Club. School program participants transition smoothly into Red or Orange groups.

## How to Enrol

School programs are coordinated through each school. Contact the school office or after-school care coordinator to register.
