# Red Group

The Red stage is perfect for young players taking their first steps in tennis.

## What We Cover

- Basic racket handling and ball control
- Introduction to forehand and backhand
- Movement and footwork fundamentals
- Fun games and activities

## Session Details

- **Age Range:** 5-8 years
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Court:** Red court (smaller)
- **Ball:** Red ball (25% compression)
