# Term 1 2026 Registration Now Open

Secure your child's spot for Term 1 2026. Registration is now open for all group programs at Foundation Tennis.

## Key Dates

- **Registration opens:** Now
- **Early bird deadline:** Check your email for specific date
- **Term 1 starts:** Week 1 of school term

## Early Bird Discount

Sign up and pay in full before the deadline to receive 10% off your term fees. This applies to all group programs.

## Sports Voucher

The $100 government sports voucher can be applied to your enrolment. Use code "VOUCHER" at checkout.

## New Players

Never been to Foundation Tennis? Book a free trial before term starts. We will find the right group for your child.

[Book a Trial](/contact)

## Returning Players

Your spot from last term is not automatically held. Please register promptly to ensure your preferred time slot.
