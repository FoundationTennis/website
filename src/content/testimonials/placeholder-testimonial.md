## Collecting Testimonials

When requesting testimonials from parents, ask:

1. What has your child enjoyed most about Foundation Tennis?
2. What improvements have you noticed in your child?
3. Why do you continue to bring your child here?
4. Would you recommend Foundation Tennis to other families?

Always get written permission before using testimonials on the website.
