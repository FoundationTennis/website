# Contact Us

Ready to start your child's tennis journey? We would love to hear from you.

## Book a Free Trial

The best way to experience Foundation Tennis is to try it. Your child can join a group lesson at no cost, with no commitment.

Just contact us with:
- Your child's name and age
- Any previous tennis experience
- Your preferred days

We will find the right group and get you booked in.

## Common Questions

**How do I sign up for a term?**
Contact us to book a trial first. Once you have found the right group, we will send you registration details.

**What if we miss a lesson?**
Let us know in advance. We can arrange a make-up lesson where possible.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
Yes. We offer flexible payment options for all programs.

**Can I claim the sports voucher?**
Absolutely. Use code "VOUCHER" at checkout to receive your $100 discount.
