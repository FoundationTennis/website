## Schedule Notes

- **Weather:** If rain cancels a lesson, we arrange a make-up or credit your account.
- **Public holidays:** No lessons on public holidays. We do not run make-ups for these dates.
- **Absences:** Please let us know if your child cannot attend. We try to offer make-up lessons where possible.
