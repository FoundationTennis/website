# About Foundation Tennis

Foundation Tennis provides professional tennis coaching in Perth, Western Australia. We offer programs for all ages and skill levels, from beginners to competitive players.

## Our Mission

To develop confident, skilled tennis players through quality coaching and a supportive learning environment.

## Our Approach

We follow the Tennis Australia Hotshots pathway for junior development, ensuring players progress through age-appropriate stages with the right equipment and court sizes.
