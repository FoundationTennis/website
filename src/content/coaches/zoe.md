# Zoe Patfull-Bulmer

Zoe is the Assistant Head Coach at Foundation Tennis and our Blind and Low Vision (BLV) Coordinator. At 19, she balances university studies with her coaching career, bringing energy and passion to every lesson.

## Her Foundation Tennis Journey

Zoe started tennis at Foundation Tennis when she was 8 years old, coached by Maxim. She grew up in the program, experiencing firsthand what it means to develop as a player here.

At 15, she began coaching. Now, she helps young players the same way she was once helped.

## What Zoe Brings

Having grown up at Foundation Tennis, Zoe understands exactly what young players experience. She remembers learning to rally, competing for the first time, and building confidence on court.

Her connection with the players shows. Kids respond to her energy and enthusiasm.

## BLV Coordinator

Zoe coordinates our Blind and Low Vision tennis program, ensuring players with visual impairments can access quality tennis coaching.

## Qualifications

- Tennis Australia Junior Development Coach
- Working with Children Check (current)
- First Aid Certificate (current)
